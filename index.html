The Anatomy of a Gem

A Gem isn't just a prompt. It's a program built from two core components.

1. Instructions (The Brain)

Role, Context, Task, Format, Tone.

2. Knowledge (The Library)

PDFs, CSVs, Brand Guidelines, Transcripts.

Gem Idea Pro-Tip: The "Rule of 3"

Look for repetitive tasks you do >3 times a week. If a task requires Specific Knowledge and a Consistent Format, it's a Gem candidate.

How to Deploy Your Gem

Follow these 4 steps to bring your new agent to life in Google Gemini.

2. Create New

Click the "New Gem" button to open the editor.
